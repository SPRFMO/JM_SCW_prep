2. Getting to Wageningen from Schiphol Airport

We strongly recommend travelling by public transport. The journey from Schiphol Airport to Wageningen by train and bus takes approximately 1 hour 30 minutes and runs frequently throughout the day.

2.1 By Public Transport (recommended)

Step 1 - Train: Schiphol Airport to Ede-Wageningen

  • Follow signs to the “Railway Station” inside the airport terminal.
  • Buy a ticket at the yellow NS vending machines or service desk. You can also pay contactless with a debit/credit card or smartphone by tapping in and out at the yellow OV-chip poles.
  • Take the Intercity (IC) train in the direction of Nijmegen. Trains run every 30 minutes. Your stop is Ede-Wageningen (the 5th station).
  • If there is no direct service, take the IC to Utrecht Centraal, change platforms, and board a train towards Arnhem/Nijmegen to Ede-Wageningen.
  • Journey time: approximately 60-75 minutes. Plan your trip at ns.nl or 9292.nl.

Step 2a - Bus to Hotel Wander (city centre)

  • From Ede-Wageningen station, exit and take the stairs down; buses depart from the bus bays outside the main entrance.
  • Take Bus 86 towards Wageningen. Alight at Wageningen, Busstation. Hotel Wander is a 5-minute walk from the bus station (Lawickse Allee 9).
  • Journey time: approximately 20-25 minutes. Buses run every 15-30 minutes.

Step 2b - Bus to Wageningen Campus Omnia (meeting venue)

  • Take Bus 303 (direction: Campus/Forum) from Ede-Wageningen station. Alight at Campus/Forum. Omnia is a short walk from the stop.
  • Journey time from Ede-Wageningen: approximately 25 minutes.
  • Tip: Use the 9292 Journey Planner or NS app for live departure times and route options.

2.2 By Taxi (alternative)

A direct taxi from Schiphol Airport to Wageningen is possible but expensive (approximately EUR 120-150 for a single ride). If you prefer this option, book in advance through a licensed taxi service.

  • For smaller groups, a taxi from Ede-Wageningen station to Hotel Wander or the campus costs approximately EUR 30-37.
  • Recommended taxi company near Ede-Wageningen station: Panda Taxi - tel. +31 (0)6 8761 7837 - pandataxi.nl (pre-booking required).

3. Daily Travel: Hotel Wander to Wageningen Campus Omnia

The distance between Hotel Wander and Omnia is approximately 2.5 km. Three options are available:

On foot

  • Walking time: approximately 28-32 minutes.
  • Follow Lawickse Allee north, continue along Binnenhaven, and follow the signs to Wageningen Campus.

By bicycle

  • Cycling time: approximately 10-12 minutes.
  • Hotel Wander offers bicycle rental for guests. The route follows the canal path and is flat throughout.
  • Wageningen is a university city with excellent cycling infrastructure.

By bus

  • Take Bus 303 (direction: Campus/Forum) from Wageningen Busstation. Alight at Campus/Forum. Travel time: approximately 10 minutes.
  • Plan your bus journey at 9292.nl/en.

This workshop is organised as part of the SPRFMO Jack Mackerel Management Strategy Evaluation (MSE) process.
